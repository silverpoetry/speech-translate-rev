# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful and productive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be considerate and constructive.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical issues.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect maintainers' time and decisions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.

Report conduct concerns through the security/support channels listed in `SECURITY.md` and `SUPPORT.md`.
